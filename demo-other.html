Other¶

The follow shows a uncategorized markup defined in reStructuredText and/or Sphinx handled by the Sphinx engine.

  • Attribution (in block quote)

  • Epigraph, Highlight and Pull-quote

  • Transition

Attribution (in block quote)¶

When a reStructuredText block quote contains an attribution, the content will be captured inside a Confluence quote block:

“It is my business to know things. That is my trade.”

—Sherlock Holmes

Epigraph, Highlight and Pull-quote¶

reStructuredText’s epigraphs, highlights and pull-quotes should be captured inside a Confluence quote block:

No matter where you go, there you are.

—Buckaroo Banzai

Transition¶

A transition (or horizontal rule) can be used to seperate content.
